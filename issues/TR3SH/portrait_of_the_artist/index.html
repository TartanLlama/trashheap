Portrait of the Artist as a Young Font

by Michael Lee Richardson

RuPaul Andre Charles
is a typeface
a series of dingbat fonts
that renders letters
as a variety of symbols.

She was originally developed
in 1990
by combining glyphs from
Lucida Icons,
Arrows
and Stars.

RuPaul Andre Charles
contains many
largely recognized
shapes and gestures,
as well as some recognized world symbols.

The patent on
RuPaul Andre Charles
the typeface
expired in 2005.
